AdMob mediation workflow

Connect your AdMob account.

Sign in with Google. The tool pulls your AdMob apps, ad units, and last-7-day metrics live from the AdMob API, then helps you build mediation waterfalls — one mediation group per source ad unit, with N labeled tier ad units as lines.

GContinue with Google

Requires AdMob API scopes: admob.readonly, admob.report, admob.monetization.

Workflow

  1. Sign in with Google (OAuth)
  2. Sync your AdMob apps + ad units
  3. Open the Builder, pick app + ad units
  4. Choose country targeting + waterfall depth
  5. Fetch live AdMob report (7 days)
  6. Tool calculates waterfall eCPM values
  7. Push: tier ad units + 1 mediation group with N waterfall lines
